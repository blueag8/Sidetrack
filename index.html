The Duo

We are a flute and guitar duo. Andrew comes from a background of playing guitar in rock bands while my background is mostly classical, having played in orchestras, concert bands and small ensembles. Both Andrew and I have worked as music teachers in a secondary school environment for many years and have some training in Jazz. We have many years of experience in performing in various groups but formed our duo which we have called “Sidetrack” late in 2017 as a response to invitations from friends and family for us to perform together.
