The Duo

Flute & Guitar

A flute and guitar duo. Andrew comes from a background of playing guitar in rock bands while Cathy's background is mostly classical, having played in orchestras, concert bands and small ensembles. Both Andrew and Cathy have worked as music teachers in a secondary school environment for many years and have some training in Jazz. They have many years of experience in performing in various groups but formed their duo which is called “Sidetrack” late in 2017 as a response to invitations from friends and family for them to perform together.
